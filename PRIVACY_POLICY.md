# Privacy Policy for StreamX Ultra

**Last Updated: December 21, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how StreamX Ultra ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

## Information We Collect

When you create an account, we may collect the following information:
- **Email Address:** Used for account creation, login, and password recovery.
- **Display Name:** The name you provide for your user profile.
- **Authentication Information:** When you sign in with third-party services like Google, Facebook, or GitHub, we receive a token to verify your identity. We do not receive or store your password for these services.

We do not collect any personal information beyond what is necessary for account functionality.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:
- To create and manage your account.
- To provide and maintain our Service.
- To authenticate you when you log in.

## Data Storage

Your account information (email, display name) is securely stored using Firebase Authentication services.

## Third-Party Services

Our Service uses third-party authentication services (Google, Facebook, GitHub). Their use of your information is governed by their respective privacy policies.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via our GitHub repository issues.
