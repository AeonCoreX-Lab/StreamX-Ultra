# Data Deletion Instructions for StreamX Ultra

**Last Updated: December 21, 2025**

We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with control over your personal data. This page provides instructions on how to request the deletion of your account and associated data from our services.

## What Data We Store

When you create an account with StreamX Ultra using Email/Password, Google, Facebook, or GitHub, we store the following information provided by the authentication service:
- Your Email Address
- Your Display Name
- A Unique User ID (generated by Firebase)

We do not store your passwords for any third-party services.

## How to Request Data Deletion

To permanently delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

1.  **Create a New GitHub Issue:**
    - Go to our official GitHub repository's "Issues" page by clicking this link: 
      **[https://github.com/cybernahid-dev/StreamX-Ultra/issues/new/choose](https://github.com/cybernahid-dev/StreamX-Ultra/issues/new/choose)**

2.  **Submit Your Request:**
    - On the "Issues" page, click the "Open" button for a new blank issue.
    - **Title:** Use the title "User Data Deletion Request".
    - **Body:** In the body of the issue, please provide the **email address** associated with the StreamX Ultra account you wish to delete.

## What Happens Next?

- Once we receive your request via the GitHub issue, we will verify your identity.
- After verification, we will permanently delete your account and all associated data from our Firebase backend.
- This process is irreversible. Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
- We will process your request within 7-14 business days and will notify you by commenting on the GitHub issue once it is complete.

---

If you have any questions, you can also mention them in the GitHub issue.
